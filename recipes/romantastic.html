Romantastic

Roma tomatoes and romano cheese make this dish extra romantic!

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 servings leftover companeasy
  • 10 medium roma tomatoes
  • 50g shredded romano cheese
  • 2 romano bean pods (optional garnish)

Directions

  1. Chop roma tomatoes and add to a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add the leftover companeasy and bring mixture to a low simmer
  3. Serve topped with romano cheese and romano bean pods
