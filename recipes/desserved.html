Desserved

Come and get your well-deserved just desserts!

Ingredients (single serving)

  • 50g of creamy strained ice material from making Childream
  • 20g of powdered sugar
  • 2 spoonfuls of sour cream
  • 1 spoonful of available fruit stems, peels or tops (apple, orange, raspberry)

Directions

  1. Combine strained ice, powdered sugar, and sour cream in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Top with fruit peels for flavour and aesthetic

    4. It almost resembles real ice cream!
