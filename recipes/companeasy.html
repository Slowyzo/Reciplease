Companeasy

A crisp, flavourful and easy meal that will always leave an impression on company!

Ingredients

  • 1000g of any raw hollow pasta
  • 6 handfuls of chopped garlic
  • The water left after rinsing one can of tomato paste (discard paste)
  • 2 handfuls of seasonal local leaves
  • 8 stalks of celery

Directions

  1. Add miscellaneous leaves to a pot with the tomato paste rinse water (1 full can)
  2. Bring to a boil, then remove the leaves by hand
  3. Divide the liquid among your serving plates
  4. Place dry paste on each plate, and set aside overnight to soak in the liquid
  5. Top with 1 - 1.5 handfuls of raw chopped garlic, and garnish with at least two celery stalks
  6. Serve and enjoy!

    7. Note: for a quicker and crunchier preparation, the soaking in step 4 can be skipped
