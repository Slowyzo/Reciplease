Childream (Chilled Cream)

A simple meal for all children and young animals

Ingredients

  • 1000mL Cream of any ruminant
  • 500g Ice or any of the common varieties of snow
  • Aphid Honey, to taste

Directions

  1. First, combine the cream with 2.3 - 1,200 drops of aphid honey (fresh)
  2. Let settle for 2 - 3 hours in a cool and quiet place
  3. Place ice material in a strainer, and prepare a chilled silver caraffe
  4. Pour the liquid mixture over the ice, and serve from the caraffe immediately

Tip: The ice material can be saved for reuse in subsequent batches of childream
